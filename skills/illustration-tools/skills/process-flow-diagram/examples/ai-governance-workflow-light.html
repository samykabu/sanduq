AI Governance Workflow

From use-case proposal through ethics review, deployment, and post-launch monitoring

Prerequisites

  • • AI policy & risk taxonomy published
  • • Ethics committee chartered with quorum
  • • Model registry / MLOps platform
  • • Incident response runbook
  • • Audit logging enabled end-to-end

Inputs → Outputs

  • • Input: Use case intake form
  • • Input: Training data lineage
  • • Output: Risk tier classification
  • • Output: Ethics review findings
  • • Output: Signed model artifact + audit trail

Tools & Integrations

  • • Risk scoring engine (internal)
  • • MLOps platform (model registry, deploy)
  • • Observability stack (drift, fairness)
  • • Audit log store (immutable)
